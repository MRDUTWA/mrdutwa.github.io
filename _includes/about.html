About Johann

I am a Graphic and Web Designer, but I see myself as my clients Brand Ambassador or as in the case of a start up a Brand Activator.

I enjoy the challenge of taking raw branding elements and making them work for a specific brand, client or target audience; Even if it means pulling a rabbit from a hat due to tight deadlines.

Over the years of broadening my knowledge of design and coding, I have developed my interpersonal skills to a stage where I can mentor and develop my fellow team members to help them reach their full potential. Knowledge is power and knowledge need to be shared.

My graphic and web designs are beautiful, usable and professional using the latest best practices prescribed in our industry.

To create my designs I utilize the Adobe Creative suite to produce and create print and digital work for a multitude of clients.

I have a passion for designing a beautiful and functional user experience, focusing on clean, elegant and efficient websites that entice the user and driving innovation and business growth.
